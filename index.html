Home Page

Sylvia's Snack Shack ltd. was established in 2016 when I noticed my colleagues eating crisps and chocolate bars as snacks.

I began making snacks for my colleagues each week.

After a few weeks, I approached my CrossFit gym and soon my delicious flapjacks were stocked in three CrossFit gyms in the Middle East!
